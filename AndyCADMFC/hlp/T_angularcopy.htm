To angular copy an object.

Angular copy, copies object similarly like the polar copy but rotates the each  copied object based on the chosen angle.

  1. Select the object to angular copy
  2. On the drawing toolbar, click on the rotation button
  3. A cross marker appears indicating the rotation center of the object
  4. To displace the center of rotation click on the cross marker, holding down the left mouse button drag the marker to desired center of rotation and release the mouse.
  5. On the transformation toolbar, click on angular copy 
  6. A dialog box pops up, enter the desired rotation angle and the number of copies and click OK or press "Enter".

 

 

 

Note: if no rotation angle is specified by displacing the cross mark. the copied objects will remain in the center.

you may select each and move as desired.
