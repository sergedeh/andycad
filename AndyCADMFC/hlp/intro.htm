Introduction

AndyCAD is a handy and easy CAD program great for beginners and professionals alike. When thinking of transforming your ideas into creative technical or mechanical drawings AndyCAD is a good choice. The features found in AndyCAD makes it a valuable tool for all kind of technical drawings.

The power of NURBS makes it a remarkable tools for artwork.  The Simple user friendly interface making it easy to learn and use. Tool tips are available as On-Screen instructions to help guide the user.

Draw different types of lines horizontal, vertical, polyline and orthogonal Line. Circles,  circle from center, circle 2 points, and circle with 3 points. Arcs, arc from center, arc 2 point, and arc 3 points.  Ellipse, B-Spline, Squares, Rectangles and Text. 

You  can easily Select objects. insert , remove/delete node , break curves/lines segments, close curves/lines segments, create chamfer or fillet, cut, copy, paste, delete and move. Undo or redo previous action. Rotate at various axes or freely, mirror, polar copy, parallel copy, rectangular array copy and angular copy. Flip objects vertically or horizontally. Send object to the background, forward, backward or front. 

You can also save drawing as .BMP. Import .BMP files. Supports Metric and English units. Print Preview (WYSIWYG) shows what the print will look like. Objects may be grouped or ungroup Create your own library. change or fill color. Easily zoom in and out of the drawing. Works with Windows 95,98,Windows NT, 2000, XP and ME  

AndyCAD is an interesting tool when creating technical drawings ranging from  electronics, mechanical, architectural, schematics, drafting, flyers, brochures, flow diagrams and artwork above all can work with your imaginations.
