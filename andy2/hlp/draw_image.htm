To Insert an image

  1. In the drawing toolbar, click
  2. Using the mouse make a selection the begin point of where you wish to place your image.
  3. Holding down the left mouse button, drag the pointer until you get the desired size of the image you wish to import.
  4. Releasing the mouse, the File dialog will popup.
  5. Choose the image file you wish to insert and
  6. Click OK.
