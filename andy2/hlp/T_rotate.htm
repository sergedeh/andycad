To rotate an object

  1. Select the object you wish to rotate
  2. On the drawing toolbar, click
  3. A cross marker appears at the center of selected object (center of rotation center )
  4. To displace the center of rotation, click on the cross marker, holding down the left mouse button, drag the cross marker to desired location (new center rotation)  and release mouse.
  5. Holding down left mouse down, dragging around rotates the object release the mouse at desired position
  6. On the drawing toolbar, click on   to leave the rotation mode.
