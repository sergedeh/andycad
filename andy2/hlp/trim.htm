Trimming Objects

To Trim a curve or line segment

  1. Click to select object you wish to trim (the object must be overlapping another object or group of objects and  have a point of intersection.
  2. Click on the modification toolbar 
  3. Click on the part of the already selected object you wish to trim  and trim as desired.
  4. Click on the ->select button to leave the trimming state.

To trim off  part of a circle against line

1. Select line as below.

2. Go step 2 above.

3. Click the part of circle you wish trim results to "After trim" below.

To trim off  the middle of a circle against  a rectangle

1.Select the rectangle in this case use the multiple select to  select all the edges of the rectangle.

2. Go to step 2 above

3. click on the part you wish to trim and trim as desired.

Note: when the trim button is toggled on you can trim multiple times.
