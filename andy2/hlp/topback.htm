Send Object to the Background or foreground

To send an object to the  front

  1. Select the object to bring to the foreground/front
  2. In the modification toolbar, click or
  3. click on the menu bar->Arrange->Send to front

To send  an object to the back

  1. Select the object you wish to send to the background
  2. In the modification toolbar, click or
  3. click on the menu bar->Arrange->Send to back
