Draw menu commands

The Draw menu offers the following commands:

Line

 

 

 

 Draw a line or polyline:
  1. Select the starting point of your line,
  2. drag the mouse till the desired endpoint.
  3. To draw a polyline, select the next starting point close to the last ending point and repeat from 2 above.
Orthogonal line Draw perpendicular line. For the drawing procedure refer to Line
Rectangle

 

 

 Draw a rectangle:
  1. Select the rectangle
  2. drag the mouse till the desired opposite corner point
Spline Draw spline. The drawing procedure follows that of the Line.
Perpendicular Line

 

 

 Draw a line perpendicular to another segment or line.
  1. Select the referential line or segment of line
  2. follow the process of Line

 
Circle

 

 

 Draw a circle, the first point is the center.
  1. Select the desired center
  2. drag the mouse till the required radius is reached
Circle 2 points

 

 

 Draw a circle passing through 2points.
  1. Select the first point on the circle
  2. drag the mouse till the required diameter is reached.
Circle 3 points

 

 

 Draw a circle passing through 3 points.
  1. Select the first point
  2. Select the second point
  3. Drag the mouse till you reached your desired third point
Arc Draw a arc, the first point is the center of the arc. Follow the Circle procedure.
Arc 2 points Draw a arc passing through two points. Follow the Circle 2 points procedure.
Arc 3 points Draw an arc passing through 3 points. Follow the Circle 3 points procedure
Text

 

 

 Draw a text.
  1. Select the starting point of your text
  2. at the end of your text drawing, press the ESC key.
